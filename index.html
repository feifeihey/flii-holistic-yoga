Our Story

Hi, I’m Feifei. My journey with FLII HOLISTIC began from a place of personal transformation. After the challenges of my last startup, I felt very much lost—until yoga, meditation, and a mindful vegan lifestyle became my anchors. They didn’t just heal me; they helped me rediscover myself.

A pivotal moment came during my 200-hour YTT in Bali. Amidst the practice, I realized: If yoga could guide me back to myself, it could do the same for others. That’s why I founded FLII HOLISTIC—to share these tools of mindfulness, movement, and holistic healing with anyone seeking their own path home.

Join me in this space where Eastern philosophy, breath, and intention weave together. You’re not just practicing yoga; you’re remembering who you are.
