Yoga

Movement, breath, and alignment — foundational practices for strength, flexibility, and inner calm.

Beginner's Hatha Yoga

Perfect for those new to yoga. Learn fundamental poses, proper alignment, and breathing techniques in a supportive environment. Build strength and flexibility at your own pace while developing body awareness and inner calm.

Restorative Yin Yoga

A gentle practice focusing on deep tissue release and meditation. Hold gentle poses for extended periods to target connective tissues and joints. Perfect for stress relief, improved flexibility, and emotional balance.

Vinyasa Flow

A dynamic practice synchronizing breath with movement. Build strength, flexibility, and endurance through flowing sequences. Suitable for those with some yoga experience who want to deepen their practice.
