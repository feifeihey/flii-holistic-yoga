Sound Healing

Resonant sound as a path to deep rest — Himalayan singing bowls and intentional listening to support the nervous system and inner harmony.

In sound healing sessions, you are invited to lie down or sit comfortably while instruments such as Himalayan singing bowls create rich, sustained vibrations. These frequencies can help quiet mental chatter, soften physical tension, and guide awareness toward stillness — not as performance, but as a gentle return to balance.

Sessions may be offered as standalone experiences or woven together with breathwork and mindful movement, depending on the offering. Booking details will appear on this page when classes are scheduled.
